37 CONFIG_LIST_FAILED (0x73)
38 Indicates that one of the core system hives cannot be linked in the
39 registry tree. The hive is valid, it was loaded OK. Examine the 2nd
40 bugcheck argument to see why the hive could not be linked in the
41 registry tree.
42 
43 PARAMETERS
44  1 - 1
45  2 - Indicates the NT status code that tripped us into thinking

48  4 - Pointer to UNICODE_STRING containing filename of hive
49 
50 DESCRIPTION
51 This can be either SAM, SECURITY, SOFTWARE or DEFAULT. One common reason
52 for this to happen is if you are out of disk space on the system drive

63 Can indicate that the SYSTEM hive loaded by the osloader/NTLDR
64 was corrupt. This is unlikely, since the osloader will check
65 a hive to make sure it isn't corrupt after loading it.

72 Repair Disk.
