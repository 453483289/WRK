664 /*++
665 
666 Routine Description:
667 
668  Walks through the list of discarded bins and attempts to
669  coalesce adjacent discarded bins into one larger bin in
670  order to satisfy an allocation request.
671 
672  It doesn't coalesce bins over the CM_VIEW_SIZE boundary.
673 
674  It doesn't coalesce bins from paged pool with bins mapped in
675  system cache views.
676
