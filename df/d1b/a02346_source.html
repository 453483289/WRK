41

43  by the FileHandle parameter. The amount of information returned is based

51  information is returned.
52

54

56 
57  Length - Supplies the length, in bytes, of the buffer.
58 
59  ReturnSingleEntry - Indicates that only a single entry should be returned
60  rather than filling the buffer with as many entries as possible.
61 
62  SidList - Optionally supplies a list of SIDs whose quota information is to
63  be returned.
64 
65  SidListLength - Supplies the length of the SID list, if one was specified.
66 
67  StartSid - Supplies an optional SID that indicates that the returned
68  information is to start with an entry other than the first. This
69  parameter is ignored if a SidList is specified.
70 
71  RestartScan - Indicates whether the scan of the quota information is to be
72  restarted from the beginning.
73 
74 Return Value:
75
